OUR SERVICES

From Sourcing
to Delivery.

From product sourcing and quality coordination to logistics support, NXTN connects the key steps required for overseas material procurement through one point of contact.

01

GLOBAL SOURCING

We identify and review overseas manufacturers and products that match the customer’s requirements and intended application.

We also support the coordination of quotations, product specifications, minimum order quantities, and production lead times.

02

LOGISTICS SUPPORT

For materials purchased through NXTN, we provide complimentary basic consultation on international shipping, estimated logistics costs, and import procedures.

When required, ocean or air freight, customs clearance, import handling, and domestic delivery can be coordinated through professional logistics partners.

03

QUALITY COORDINATION

We support clear communication between customers and manufacturers from product specification review to production and delivery.

When quality-related matters arise, NXTN assists with issue communication, follow-up, and practical coordination with the manufacturer.
